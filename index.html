About

After assembling engines for Ford Motor Company for seven years,
I attended the welding program at Apollo Career Center where with
the help of several talented instructors i developed the foundation of
skills i have today. From this experience i was offered a position at
General Dynamics Land Systems, where we weld and assemble the
Abrams battle tank and Stryker vehicles for the United States military.
Here i've learned and obtained certifications for welding with various
types of wire and operating several robotic welding machines.
